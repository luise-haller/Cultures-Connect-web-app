Did you know that more than 3,800 cultures exist worldwide? That’s a lot!
Sadly, in today’s age, the average person only knows around 120 of these, roughly translating to 3.2% . This dilemma frequently leads to individuals having an alienated view of people with cultures unbeknownst to them, prompting a less compassionate world. CulturesConnect hopes to solve this problem by offering an all-around tool to anyone wishing to learn more about the existing cultures, especially non-mainstream ones.
