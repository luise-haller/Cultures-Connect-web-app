Did you know that more than 3,800 cultures exist worldwide?...
That's a lot! But even more surprising is that the average person only knows about 3.2% of these cultures. Not only has this deprived them of all the valuable knowledge gathered across different cultures, but it has also created a culturally-divided society where hatred and disunity have become the norm.

CulturesConnect hopes to solve this problem by offering an all-around tool to anyone wishing to learn more about existing cultures, especially non-mainstream ones.
